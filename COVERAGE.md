# Coverage

This document describes the data coverage of the `maltese-competition-mcp` server.

## Authority

**MCCAA — Malta Competition and Consumer Affairs Authority**
Official portal: https://www.mccaa.org.mt/

## Decisions

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `abuse_of_dominance` | Abuse of dominant position under Article 9, Competition Act (Cap. 379) |
| `cartel` | Anti-competitive agreements under Article 5, Competition Act (Cap. 379) |
| `sector_inquiry` | Formal sector inquiries by MCCAA |
| `merger` | Merger control decisions notified under the Competition Act |

## Mergers

Merger control decisions — Phase I clearance, Phase I with conditions, and Phase II investigations.

## Sectors Covered

- Financial services
- Gaming
- Telecommunications
- Retail
- Tourism
- Construction
- Media

## Data Currency

- Last ingest: 2026-03-23
- Updates: periodic (see `check-freshness.yml` workflow)

## Gaps and Limitations

- Coverage extends to publicly available MCCAA decisions only
- Informal settlements and unpublished decisions are not included
- Data age may lag official publications — always verify against primary sources
